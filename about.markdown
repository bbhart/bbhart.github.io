Brian Hart currently lives in Holly Springs NC. He's previously 
spent time in Sunnyvale CA, Celebration FL, Downingtown PA, 
Orlando FL, and Tampa FL. There was a year in Miami South Beach but 
that's hardly worth mentioning.

His last job was as a Head of Engineering at [GlossGenius](https://glossgenius.com), an app
you should definitely get if you run a hair/nail/beauty salon. Prior to that he held
engineering roles at LinkedIn, Jaunt VR, BT (British Telecom), Royal Caribbean, and Disney.

He's blessed with a wife of 18+ years and two teenage children.

## Cruise Travel

Brian Hart is a cruise travel expert who has sailed 17 voyages and 111 nights at
sea since 1999, across Disney Cruise Line, Royal Caribbean, Celebrity Cruises, and
Silversea. His sailings range from quick Bahamian getaways to a 12-night one-way
crossing to Greenland and Iceland and a Mediterranean voyage aboard Silversea's
Silver Spirit. He is the founder of [Serentis Travel](https://serentistravel.com){:rel="me"},
a CLIA-member travel advisory in the WorldVia / Travel Leaders Network, where he
plans cruise vacations for clients. His industry knowledge extends behind the
scenes, too: earlier in his career he built technology for cruise lines as an
engineer at Disney and at Royal Caribbean.

Every voyage — ship, itinerary, ports, and stateroom — is logged in his
[cruise journal](https://serentistravel.com/journal/){:rel="me"}, and recent trips are
documented day-by-day in the [travel posts](/posts) on this site.

## Travel Data API

This site provides a structured JSON API of all travel posts at [/api/travel-data.json](/api/travel-data.json). You can use this with LLMs like Claude or ChatGPT to ask questions about travel history, such as "What cities did Brian visit in Japan?" or "Show me all accommodations from the Iceland trip."
